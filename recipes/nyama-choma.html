Nyama Choma (Roast Meat)

Description

The name comprises of two swahili words; nyama meaning meat and choma meaning roast. Thus translated as Roast Meat. It is a popular dish in East Africa, especially in Kenya and Tanzania. Beef or goat meat are used.

Ingredients

  • 2 Kg goat or beef meat
  • Cooking oil
  • 4 Cups warm water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare your grill.
  2. Toss the meat with the oil, then thread it on skewers. Stir the salt into the warm water until it is fully dissolved.
  3. Grill the skewered meat, basting it from time to time with the saltwater until it is cooked to your desired taste
  4. Remove the meat from the skewers.
  5. Chop the meat into bite-sized chunks
  6. Serve with kachumbari and ugali.
