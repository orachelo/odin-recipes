Kachumbari (Kenyan Tomato and Onion Salad)

Description

Kachumbari is a refreshing and delicious Kenyan and East-African salad comprising tomatoes, onion and lemon juice.

It is used as a condiment and served with a meal of nyama choma, French Fries among others.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables: Fresh tomato, red onions
  • Spices: Cayenne pepper, black pepper, and salt.
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Soak the raw onions in 2 cups of water with 1 teaspoon of salt for 10-15 minutes.
  2. While waiting for the onions, cut the tomatoes and chop the cilantro. Drain the onions and pat dry.
  3. Add the vegetables in a large mixing bowl. Then add the salt, pepper, and juice from the lemon. Mix everything together.
  4. Garnish with cilantro if desired.
